Your 7 Essential Items

  • Digital Thermometer (for accurate fever checks)
  • Paracetamol Syrup or Tablets
  • Antiseptic Solution (like Pyodine)
  • ORS Sachets (Nimkol for dehydration)
  • Adhesive Bandages (various sizes)

  • Sterile Gauze and Tape
