Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery timings in Kotla?

We deliver from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Most orders within Kotla Arab Ali Khan are delivered within 60 to 90 minutes.

How do I send my prescription?

You can use the "Order via Prescription" button on our homepage to upload a photo, or send it directly to our WhatsApp at +92 332 958 8433.

Are your medicines authentic?

Yes, 100%. We source all our medicines directly from authorized distributors and multinational pharmaceutical companies like GSK, Abbott, and Pfizer.

Can I return a medicine?

For safety reasons, refrigerated medicines (like Insulin) cannot be returned. Other items can be returned within 24 hours if the seal is unbroken and you have the original receipt.

Still need help?

Our pharmacists are available 24/7 on WhatsApp for any medical queries.
