Privacy Policy

Your health information is personal. We are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact us via WhatsApp to reserve medicine for pickup, we may collect the following information:

  • Your Name and Phone Number.
  • Medicine names and quantities you wish to reserve.
  • Photos of doctor prescriptions (for regulated medicines).

2. How We Use Your Data

We only use your information to facilitate your in-store pickup. Specifically:

  • To verify the availability of medicines in our Kotla branch.
  • To confirm your identity when you arrive for pickup.
  • To provide pharmacological guidance via WhatsApp.

3. Data Security & Prescriptions

We treat prescription photos with the highest level of confidentiality. These photos are only accessible by our licensed Category-A Pharmacist and are never shared with third parties or advertisers.

4. WhatsApp Communication

By clicking our WhatsApp buttons, you initiate a chat with us. WhatsApp uses end-to-end encryption to protect your messages. However, we advise users to clear their sensitive chat history periodically for their own personal security.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Sharif Pharmacy does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We do not use your phone number for automated marketing or spam.

If you have any questions about your data, feel free to ask us.
