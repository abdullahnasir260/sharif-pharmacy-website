1. In-Store Pickup Only

2. Prescription Requirements

In compliance with Punjab Healthcare Commission regulations, "Schedule G" and other controlled medicines will not be issued without a valid prescription from a registered medical practitioner (PMDC). We reserve the right to refuse any order if a valid prescription is not provided at the counter.

4. Pricing & Availability

Medicine prices are subject to change as per Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan (DRAP) notifications. We strive to keep our catalog updated, but availability may vary. We will confirm the stock and current price via WhatsApp once you place your reservation.

For safety and hygiene, medicines cannot be returned or exchanged once they leave the pharmacy premises. Refrigerated items (like Insulin) and life-saving drugs are strictly non-returnable under any circumstances.
