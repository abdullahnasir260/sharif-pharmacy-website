Healthy Skin This Winter

Expert Winter Care Advice from Sharif Pharmacy

Winter in Kotla can strip your skin of its natural moisture. Indoor heating and cold winds lead to dryness and irritation. Follow our professional guide to keep your skin supple and glowing.

Lukewarm Water Only

Hot showers strip natural oils. Switch to lukewarm water to prevent your skin from becoming tight and dry.

Lock in Moisture

Apply moisturizer immediately after washing while your skin is still damp to trap moisture into the cells.

Don't Skip SPF

UV rays are still powerful in winter. Always wear sun protection to prevent hidden skin damage and aging.

Humidify Your Room

Central heating dries the air. Using a humidifier at night adds essential moisture back to your environment.
